★★★★★ Liked Watched by tootifruty 07 Nov 2025

After waiting an entire year—because I had chosen to watch Oppenheimer instead of Barbie at the cinema—
I CAN FINALLY SAY IT WAS WORTH ALL THE ATTENTION.
The movie felt so fine, so real, and so me.
I’m honestly glad I watched it at 18, right at the end of my teenage years, just as I was about to turn 19.
It spoke to me in a way I didn’t expect; it taught me so much, and I truly love it from the bottom of my heart.
It opened my eyes to how “dad-rules” shape the world, and how things should be easier for us girls.
